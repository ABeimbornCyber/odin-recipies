Prawns

This is a mediocre recipe for making prawns

Ingredients:

  • Prawns
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Skin the prawns
  2. Bring the water to a rolling boil
  3. Throw the prawns in for 5 minutes
  4. Remove the prawns from the boiling water
  5. Season prawns and enjoy!
