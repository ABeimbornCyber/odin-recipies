Eggs

This is a simple recipe for scrambled eggs!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Melt the butter in your frying pan
  2. Beat eggs in a bowl
  3. Add eggs to pan, stir while cooking
  4. When the eggs cease to be shiny, remove them from the pan
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Enjoy
