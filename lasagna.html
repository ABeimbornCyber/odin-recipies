Lasagna

This lasagna recipe will change your life! Just ask my son, the first time that he had a bite of this original Italian lasagna his life was changed. He went from being just a normal son to being a super son! He lived on to become one of the world's best humans, even getting a "Best Human" award. It was amazing, he became really successful, from rags to riches, effectively!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzerela Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • A whole lot of love!

Steps:

  1. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  2. While doing step 1, Brown the ground beef
  3. Layer the bottom of the pan with noodles
  4. Add some sauce on top of the bottom layer
  5. Add cheese
  6. Add anothe layer of noodles
  7. Repeat the last 3 steps as much as you would like!
  8. Top off the lasagna with one last layer of Mozzerela
  9. Pop the pan in the oven @ 350 degrees Fahrenheit, and leave it there for 45 minutes
  10. Enjoy!
